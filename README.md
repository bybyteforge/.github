## 🌟 Flagship Product

### Kinderlinc

**Kinderlinc** is our flagship SaaS platform built specifically for early childhood education centres, including taska, tadika, kindergartens, and childcare providers.

### Features

- 👨‍👩‍👧 Parent mobile application
- 👩‍🏫 Teacher management portal
- 🏫 Centre administration dashboard
- 📅 Attendance & QR check-in
- 💳 Billing & payments
- 📸 Daily activity timeline
- 🍽 Meal tracking
- 😴 Nap tracking
- 💊 Medication management
- 🩺 Health & temperature screening
- 📊 Business analytics
- 🔔 Push notifications
- 📄 Digital document management
- 🤖 AI-powered features (planned)
